About

Welcome to Changing Concrete. The goal of this website is to provide information about concrete and its environmental impacts as well as future developments in concrete.

Why concrete?

Concrete is more than just a building material; it’s a cornerstone of modern civilization. From the roads we drive on to the homes we live in, concrete is integral to our daily lives. Understanding its properties, history, and future developments can help us make better choices for sustainable construction and environmental stewardship.

Who is this for?

This website is designed for anyone interested in learning about concrete. This includes students, researchers, and anyone curious about this essential building material.

What information is there?

  • Introduction to Concrete: Basics of concrete, its composition, and properties.
  • History of Concrete: Evolution of concrete from ancient times to the present.
  • Future of Concrete: Innovations and trends shaping the future of concrete.
  • Environmental Impact: Environmental considerations and sustainable practices in concrete production and use.
